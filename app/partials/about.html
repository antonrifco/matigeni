About Matigeni

Tell me about that.

Imagine that we can raise people's awareness about something bad happening on earth by playing location guessing game.

We will build a website with chat functionality in which participants can play guessing game for specific locations on earth. Locations will be carefully selected that feature sites that were (heavily) damaged because of human activities (chemical spill, forest burning, over exploration, etc.).

At the end of each round, it will feature more information about the incidents.

The Project

Matigeni was developed during NASA SpaceApps Challenge 2014 hackathon. The author, Anton (mail to him at anton.rifco[at]gmail.com), joined the competition virtually from Dubai

Literally, Matigeni means "Turn off the Fire".

But, it's commonly used in Indonesia to refer to ancient ritual of not doing anything for few days. And, by that, it means you're fasting, should not sleep, and just stay at home doing nothing. It is done to get closer to "Sang Hyang", the God Almighty.

The name, Matigeni, inspired us to stop doing nothing for the sake of our beloved planet earth.

This project was only the initial concept. We hope that the idea will grow until people become aware and change their behavior to be more "Earth friendly". Even if it will take a lifetime to do that.
